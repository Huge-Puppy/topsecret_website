FAQ

How many people can I have on my team?

You can have as many team members as you'd like. Every team member will get points for clues you find.

What will I need to bring with me?

You'll need a smartphone to scan the signs and to get clues from the website. A pencil and paper will help towards solving the clues.

What am I looking for?

You are looking for small, colored signs with our diamond logo on them. They could be out in the open or hidden in a treasure chest. See this picture. There will be a QR code on the signs.

How far away will the clues be?

You can find the location in the hunt description. All the clues will be within the specified region. For example, if the treasure hunt is in Logan, UT, all of the clues will be within the borders of Logan, UT.

Can children participate?

Kids really enjoy looking for signs in our treasure hunts! They will be able to participate more in solving clues if you do a treasure hunt with a low difficulty.

How long will a treasure hunt take?

A hunt with 5 clues takes on average about 1.5 hours. This varies based on group size and hunt difficulty.

Do you do events?

Yes! We host treasure hunts for birthdays, corporate events, parties... you name it! Contact alpha@topsecrethunts.com for more info.

Is this a great date night?

Absolutely! Cheap and original. Your date will love a treasure hunt!

How do I prepare for a treasure hunt?

Re-watch national treasure, practice your code breaking skills, and say a prayer.
